Intro

Hi! I'm Britney, or Brit if you prefer! I am an aspiring Web Developer learning all there is to know about coding and programming at Lambda School with their wonderful Full Stack Web Development course. Before Lambda the only coding experience I had was cheat codes for video games, specifically The Sims 2-4. Unless you count MySpace days where everyone was creating cool profile designs and embedded music links. That being said, I have already learned so much in my first 3 weeks and am quite proud of what I am accomplishing and have accomplished so far! From passing my first Sprint Challenge with flying colors, to beginning creation of my own portfolio site designed completely by me, I am loving learning to code and am so happy to have found this amazing opportunity with Lambda School! About

About who I am

Born in Adel, GA and raised in Seale, AL, I am a southern woman with big dreams. I've been through many things in life but I refuse to let it define who I am like it did for many years. I have broken the chains of the past and I am embracing life as it comes, while creating a better future for myself and my family (which includes my significant other and our furbabies)..

Present

Currently I am a Full Stack Web Development student at Lambda School. When I am not in class I can be found working my full time job as a remote tech support advisor, practicing code, or relaxing with my significant other and our furbies. I am interviewing for a promotion within my current job soon and am confident that I will do well. I love writing, photography, reading, and relaxing every chance I get (What's sleep anyhow??)

Past

I went through a few different colleges after high school but they didn't work out. Through all the years I knew I wanted to return to school and complete a degree or crtificate program that would help me have a better career, better life, and better financial stability. With the crazy schedules I kept, nothing ever seemed to fit into my crazy schedules or budget and I could not find a program that made me excited to take the plunge. Then I stumbled upon during a scroll on Facbook. Creating websites & digital art has always fascinated me, so I know I have finally landed somewhere that will help me meet my full potential and goals!

Future

I've only just begun my journey at Lambda and I am already super stoked to complete the program and earn my endorsment! I have big dreams for the future, which include relocating out of Alabama! I'm not 100% sure where this career will take me, but I know that the future is bright and filled with lots and lots of code, thanks to ! I look forward to embracing the amazing thinks coming my way!
